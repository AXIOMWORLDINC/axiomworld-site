Vendor and Identity Fraud Detection

AI models trained on behavioral signals, document metadata, and registration patterns flag anomalous vendor identities before they enter the procurement chain. Predictive Compliance Scoring

The rules engine handles explicit compliance. The AI layer adds predictive compliance — flagging transactions that are technically within the rules but statistically consistent with fraud. A procurement that clears every rule but follows the pattern of a corrupt tender gets flagged at the point of submission. Not discovered in an audit two years later.
